About Us

Fin – Soft & Associates is a Leading Financial and ERP Consulting Firm incorporated in 2010. It engages mainly in the provision of Accounting/ERP Software Solutions, Audit, Tax and Information Technology Services in order to promote operational efficiency of financial movement in an organization. Our dedication to the three underlying principles of professionalism, responsiveness and quality differentiate us from the rest.

Our company, which consists of group of Chartered Accountants, Chartered Financial Analysts and Information Technology Specialists, is set to providing business advisory and Accounting/ERP Software Development, Sales, Upgrades, Implementation, Training and Support of accounting system to a wide range of business and individuals.

Our Vision To be the main company synonymous with Accounting Software Development, Sales, Implementation, Training and Maintenance of Computerized Accounting Software for organizations seeking Accounting Solutions and other corporate challenges”.
Our Mission To become the leading Financial & I.T Consulting firm that will nurture Organizations through quality provision and training of computerized Accounting Software for proper accountability, profitability and accuracy of accounting records.
