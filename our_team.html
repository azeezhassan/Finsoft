Our Team

A.A. AdeyemiManaging Partner / Principal Consultant

Adeyemi holds B.Sc. and MBA Accounting & Finance from Lagos State University, Ojo, Lagos. He is an Associate member of Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN) and Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) respectively. He launched his professional career as an Accountant at Oluseyi Oderinde & Co. (Chartered Accountants) now SAO & Co Professional Services, one of the fastest growing Audit firms in Nigeria where he was exposed to accounting system of various organizations. Thereafter he leaped into the ever challenging corporate world and worked in various capacities with leading companies in different sectors of the economy such as Manufacturing, Information Technology and Construction. During these period, he developed competence in the areas of Auditing, Accounting, I.T and Management. He is a Certified ERP Consultant. His broad knowledge puts him in a position to render qualitative professional services beyond the traditional accounting.

Loh Siew MengAssociate Partner

As an Associate Partner, Meng serves as a growth catalyst, helping to establish strategic direction for firms and providing consulting services to clients. He has over 35years experience in Information Technology and Management. Mend brings global experience, both as a corporate leader, as well as a consultant. He has advised hundreds of banks and vendors on business and technology strategies. Prior to joining Fin-Soft & Associates, he served as Vice President of Informatics Academy in Singapore and Rector of Katsina State Institute of Technology and Management.
